About Me

Hello, my name is Kingsley Chibuikem Chife, but I go by the nickname Rawfile. I am a photographer from Awka-South in Anambra state, Nigeria, and I started my photography journey in March 2018.

I have had the opportunity to work at some amazing studios in the region. I started my career at G24 Studios in 2018 and worked there until 2019, before moving on to De Prime Studios where I worked until 2020. I then joined Velox Studio as a Photographer and Manager from 2020 to 2022. I am currently a freelancer, focusing on providing high-quality photography services to clients.

My education started at Udeozo Memorial Primary School, where I attended from 2000 to 2009. I then attended Comprehensive Secondary School, where I obtained my WAEC (West African Examination Council Certificate) in 2015. Currently, I am studying Computer Science at Nnamdi Azikiwe University and learning to become a full stack developer.

Outside of photography and coding, I enjoy reading and exploring new opportunities to learn and grow. I am passionate about becoming a full stack developer and pursuing a career in tech.

If you're interested in working with me, please feel free to contact me at codebyrawfile@gmail.com.
